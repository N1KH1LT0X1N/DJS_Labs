+============================================+
|           SUMMARY & CONCLUSIONS           |
+============================================+

Selection Sort:
  - Predictable O(n┬▓) performance
  - Works well on small datasets
  - Minimal extra space needed
  - Better for memory-constrained systems

Quick Sort:
  - Average O(n log n) performance
  - Much faster on large datasets
  - Worst case O(n┬▓) with poor pivot selection
  - Industry standard for general-purpose sorting

Recommendation:
  Use Quick Sort for better average performance.
  Use Selection Sort for educational purposes or very small arrays.
